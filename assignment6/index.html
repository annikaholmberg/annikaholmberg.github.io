JNL 221: Data Visualization Assignment

The New York City Police Department, better known as NYPD, has been very transparent in hate crime reporting, even setting up websites to report hate crime incidents. This set of data, from OpenData NYC, tracks hate crime reports responded to by the NYPD from 2019-2022. As seen in the data, crimes of anti-Semetic nature have the highest incident reports in comparison to the other demographics, with hate crimes regarding race coming behind. Anti-Semetic crimes have declined in 2022, but that does not make up for the staggeringly high number of crimes that have already occured. All types of crime experienced a drop in 2020, aside from gender, which may be correlated to the pandemic and further isolation. With social activism and movements increasing over the years, society calls for an end to hate crimes.
